About us

We are a community of programmers that want to make the world a better place. We believe that coding can be a creative tool to make things that can help everyone all over the world, for free. Do you have an idea that you think is worth making to help someone? Do you want to help make tools that could change someones lives for good? Join Us! Here in Ikigai Coders we think that everyone can make a change.

Proyects

Here are some of the proyects that we are working on!

Hover for more info
Communicate Without Barriers

It's an app that can translate in sign lenguage what the user wants to communicate. You can also take photo of the sign lenguage and it will translate it to words.

Volunteers Around Me

This is a proyect that tracks the volunteer work that can be done around you and guides you on the process to getting in touch with them.

Healing Minds

Healing Minds helps connect mental health professionals with you for free! Or you can chat with an AI that could talk to you and keep up with your mental health.
